IPAS Challenge

2021

Team Aero successfully participated in 30 day long International Planetary Aerial Vehicle Systems Challenge 2021 which was organised by MSSA (Mars Society South Asia) in which teams participated from around the worldwide. We are glad to announce that we secured 12 rank internationally.

It was a simulated completion, amid pandemic in which the teams had to design an astronaut - assistive UAV i.e. functional in Martian atmosphere and can perform certain operations and tasks like analysis of terrain and atmosphere . In our project the Mars UAV, which is a technology demonstration to test powered, controlled flight and performed tasks on another world. The design of this UAV with the possibility to use for transfer payloads, process images and collect soil sample will give higher mission productivity. Future space exploration requires close study of extra-terrestrial bodies such as Mars. We prepared EDR report which includes all we have done.

The competition results was judged only on the merit of Engineering Design Report (EDR) with no further rounds.
It was a great overall experience and the team got a great opportunity to work on its ingenious and creative aspects.
